---
title: Members and roles
description: Manage team members in your Dify workspace and understand what each role can do.
---

Dify uses role-based access control within each workspace. Every member of a workspace is assigned exactly one role, which determines what they can view and modify.

## Roles

### Owner

The account that created the workspace. There is exactly one owner per workspace.

- Full access to all workspace settings, applications, and knowledge bases
- Can invite and remove members
- Can change any member's role
- Can transfer ownership to another member
- Can access and modify billing settings

### Admin

Trusted collaborators with broad management access.

- Can create, edit, and delete applications and knowledge bases
- Can manage model providers
- Can invite and remove members
- Can change member roles (but cannot change the owner's role or promote someone to owner)
- Cannot access billing settings

### Normal member

Standard team members.

- Can create and edit applications and knowledge bases
- Can view workspace members
- Cannot manage model providers or workspace settings
- Cannot invite or remove members

### Dataset operator

A specialized role scoped to knowledge base management only.

- Can create, edit, and delete knowledge bases and documents
- Cannot create or edit applications
- Cannot manage workspace settings or members

## Role permissions matrix

| Action | Owner | Admin | Normal | Dataset operator |
|--------|-------|-------|--------|------------------|
| Create applications | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Delete applications | ✓ | ✓ | — | — |
| Manage model providers | ✓ | ✓ | — | — |
| Create/edit knowledge bases | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Delete knowledge bases | ✓ | ✓ | — | ✓ |
| View members | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Invite members | ✓ | ✓ | — | — |
| Remove members | ✓ | ✓ | — | — |
| Change member roles | ✓ | ✓ | — | — |
| Workspace settings | ✓ | — | — | — |
| Billing settings | ✓ | — | — | — |
| Transfer ownership | ✓ | — | — | — |

## Inviting members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open member settings">
    Go to **Settings → Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Click the **Invite Member** button in the upper right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email addresses">
    Enter one or more email addresses. You can invite multiple people at once by separating addresses with commas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a role">
    Choose the role to assign: Admin, Normal, or Dataset Operator. You cannot invite someone as Owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitations">
    Click **Send**. Dify sends each invitee an email with an activation link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Invitation links expire after **72 hours** by default (configurable via `INVITE_EXPIRY_HOURS` on self-hosted deployments).

<Note>
If the invited email address already has a Dify account, the person will be added to the workspace immediately. If not, they must complete account registration via the invitation link.
</Note>

## Removing members

1. Go to **Settings → Members**.
2. Find the member in the list.
3. Click the **Remove** (trash) icon next to their name.
4. Confirm the removal.

Removing a member revokes their access to the workspace immediately. It does not delete their Dify account—they can still access other workspaces they belong to.

<Warning>
You cannot remove yourself from a workspace if you are the owner. Transfer ownership first.
</Warning>

## Changing a member's role

1. Go to **Settings → Members**.
2. Click the role badge next to the member's name.
3. Select the new role from the dropdown.
4. The change takes effect immediately.

## Transferring workspace ownership

Ownership transfer is a two-step verified process to prevent accidental transfers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate the transfer">
    Go to **Settings → Members** and click **Transfer Ownership**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive and enter the confirmation code">
    Dify sends a confirmation code to the current owner's email address. Enter the code and the one-time token to verify your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the new owner">
    Choose the member to transfer ownership to. They must already be a member of the workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Submit the transfer. The selected member becomes the new Owner and the previous owner becomes an Admin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Both the outgoing owner and the new owner receive notification emails when a transfer completes.

<Info>
Ownership transfer may be restricted on enterprise deployments. If the **Transfer Ownership** button is not visible, contact your workspace administrator.
</Info>
